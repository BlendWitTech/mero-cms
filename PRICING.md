# Mero CMS — Pricing

Mero CMS ships as a **one-time-purchase product** — no monthly fees, no subscriptions. Licenses are split into two families (Personal and Organizational) with four tiers each. Prices below are in NPR.

All tiers include lifetime updates to the same major version and one year of support at the listed level.

---

## Personal

For solo creators, freelancers, and personal brands.

### Basic — **Rs 20,000**
Essential tools to get your personal site online.

- 1 Starter Theme
- Blog (posts, categories, tags)
- Theme-related sections
- Menu & section content management
- Basic branding & settings
- Basic SEO (titles, meta descriptions)
- 5 GB storage
- Email support
- _Plugin Marketplace is not included on this tier._

### Premium — **Rs 35,000**  (recommended)
More themes, more power — with full SEO, analytics, forms, and webhooks.

- Up to 3 Themes
- Plugin Marketplace (individual plugins purchased separately)
- Full branding & settings control
- Theme sections & DB content management (menus & sections)
- Complete SEO suite
- Analytics integration
- Audit log
- Full Site Editor & Site Pages
- Code-level theme editing (edit theme source from the admin, WordPress-style)
- Forms (builder + submission inbox)
- Webhooks (notify Slack / Zapier / anything on CMS events)
- Visual Theme Editor — available as a marketplace add-on
- 20 GB storage
- Priority support

### Professional — **Rs 65,000 – Rs 85,000**
Up to 5 themes, visual editor, white-label, and API access.

- Up to 5 Themes from our portfolio
- Everything in Premium
- Collections (custom content types)
- Visual Theme Editor — pick a section, choose a supported component variant, swap it live
- White-label (hide "Powered by Mero")
- API access (REST + scoped API keys)
- AI Studio
- Website analytics dashboard inside the CMS
- Complete settings suite
- 100 GB storage
- Dedicated support

### Custom — **Rs 100,000+**
A fully tailored personal package built around your exact needs: unlimited themes, custom development scope, custom integrations, dedicated infrastructure, on-premise deployment, 24/7 support, onboarding & training.
