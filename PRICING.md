# Mero CMS — Pricing

Mero CMS is available under two options. Both are one-time payments — no monthly fees, no subscriptions.

---

## Option 1 — CMS License

**Who it's for:** Teams or developers who want to self-host the CMS and build or bring their own theme.

**What you get:**
- Full Mero CMS source code (NestJS backend + Next.js admin dashboard)
- All content modules: blogs, pages, menus, services, team, testimonials, leads, media, SEO, analytics, themes
- Theme upload system (upload any ZIP theme)
- Role-based access control
- Setup wizard
- 1 year of source code updates
- Deployment guide (Railway + Vercel)
- Email support

**Price:** Contact us — hello@blendwit.com or blendwit.com/mero-cms/pricing

---

## Option 2 — CMS + Custom Theme Development

**Who it's for:** Businesses who want a complete, ready-to-launch website — we handle the CMS setup and build a custom theme designed for your brand.

**What you get (everything in Option 1, plus):**
- Custom Next.js theme designed for your brand
- Theme built with Tailwind CSS
- Theme seeded with your real content (menus, posts, services, testimonials)
- Deployed to Railway (backend) + Vercel (frontend)
- Theme ZIP packaged for re-upload or reuse
- Up to 2 revision rounds
- Priority email support for 90 days
- 30-minute onboarding call

**Price:** Contact us — hello@blendwit.com or blendwit.com/contact
