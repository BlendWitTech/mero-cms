# Contributing to Mero CMS

Mero CMS is a proprietary product owned by **Blendwit Tech**. Contributions are by invitation only.

## How to Become a Contributor

1. Apply at: https://blendwit.com/mero-cms/contribute
2. Once approved, you will receive a GitHub collaborator invite
3. Sign the Contributor Agreement sent to your email
4. Then follow the setup instructions in [SETUP.md](SETUP.md)

## Contribution Rules

- All contributions must be made via **Pull Requests** — no direct pushes to `main` or `develop`
- PRs must pass all CI checks before review
- PRs must be reviewed and approved by a Blendwit Tech maintainer
- All contributed code becomes the property of Blendwit Tech under the terms of the Contributor Agreement
- Do not share, distribute, or discuss internal implementation details outside approved channels

## Reporting Issues

Approved contributors may open issues directly in this repository.

For all others: contact hello@blendwit.com
